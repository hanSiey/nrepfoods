About

NREP Consulting (Pty) Ltd ("NREP") is a contemporary catering company founded and managed by a seasoned chef, Simangele Nomvula Radebe. NREP is a 100% black female owned catering company, which provides create, innovative, progressive and accurate solutions to its clients through the provision of tailored catering services.

We prepare specialized meals, we shop for meal ingredients and select the freshest ingredients possible and try to know the charateristics of every ingredient that we use. We use cooking techniques such as baking, frying, roasting, grilling, steaming, poaching, simmering, broiling, etc. We laso invest in cooking tools and kitchen equipment. As we strive for diversity both on our menu and inour staff, we are prepared to work in a dynamic changing enviroment. We encourage creativy and constructive contributions from vendors, staff, and customers. We are willing to think outside the box convention canteen success.

NREP Consulting (Pty) Ltd is a BBBEE status level 3 contribution certified enterprise.
